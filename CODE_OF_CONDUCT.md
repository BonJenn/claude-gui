# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

Blackcrab should be a practical, respectful place to collaborate. Participants
are expected to:

- Be direct without being hostile.
- Assume good faith, while still asking for evidence and clear reasoning.
- Keep feedback focused on the work.
- Respect different experience levels and backgrounds.
- Accept maintainer decisions once a decision is made.

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, insults, threats, sexualized
language or imagery, sustained disruption, doxxing, or other conduct that makes
the project unsafe or unproductive.

## Enforcement

The maintainer may edit or delete comments, close issues, reject contributions,
temporarily block participation, or permanently ban participants who violate
this code.

If you need to report a conduct issue, contact the maintainer privately through
GitHub. Do not use public issues for sensitive conduct reports.
